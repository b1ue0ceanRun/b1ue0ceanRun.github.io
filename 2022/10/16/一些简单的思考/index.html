最近看S12LOL全球总决赛一些简单的思考

Posted on 2022-10-16 In 人生感悟

Firstly, I will put my favorite video about S12 lol final here.

这一刻我即天选！

Today, I have watched the competition about TES, it’s a pity that TES lost the match with GAM. Even through they defeat DRX and Rouge after that, their journey in S12 final is over. In bilibili, many video and comments started to mock and criticize TES’s coaches and players, but I have my own opinions.

The first thing I want to say is that no one can guarantee that they will be able to play 100% in the game like they do in their daily training. Everyone can make mistakes. Through they lost the chance to qualify for the group in the end and the chance to fight for campion, they still fight to the last second. I think this spirit is worthy of praise. Sometimes, thing won’t go right as people’s prediction and expectation, that’s the fascination of competition!

I have the similar experience. In high school, I have thought I was able to win the first prize in our province, but I only won the third price at last. The same thing happened to me in the college entrance examination, Judging by my usual exam results, it’s pretty easy for me to go to a reputable 985 university, but I screwed it and finally went to an ordinary 211 university. I was so depress for a long time, but I had to accept the result. For TES players and also for me, the effort and sweat they put in no one can see and others only see the result. Nobody can always win, so please respect them! Everyone fight for champion is a hero.

Those who have experienced defeat and still fight for the champion are always worthy of praise!
